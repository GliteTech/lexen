# Code Of Conduct

lexEN discussions should stay professional, evidence-based, and focused on improving benchmark
quality.

## Expected Conduct

Participants are expected to:

* treat contributors, reviewers, and users respectfully;
* discuss label disagreements with concrete linguistic evidence;
* separate criticism of an annotation decision from criticism of a person;
* avoid harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language;
* protect sensitive information and report accidental exposure privately.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainers at `info@glite.ai`. For sensitive data exposure or
security concerns, follow [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) instead of opening a public issue.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that are inconsistent with these
expectations.
