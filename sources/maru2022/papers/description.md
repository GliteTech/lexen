# Maru 2022 Paper Sources

This directory contains the primary paper for the Maru et al. 2022 WSD hard benchmark used as the
base source for lexEN.

## Included Files

* `maru-etal-2022-nibbling-hard-core-wsd.pdf` - ACL Anthology PDF.
* `maru-etal-2022-nibbling-hard-core-wsd.bib` - ACL Anthology BibTeX citation.

## Paper

Marco Maru, Simone Conia, Michele Bevilacqua, and Roberto Navigli. 2022. "Nibbling at the Hard Core
of Word Sense Disambiguation." In Proceedings of the 60th Annual Meeting of the Association for
Computational Linguistics (Volume 1: Long Papers), pages 4724-4737, Dublin, Ireland. Association for
Computational Linguistics.

ACL Anthology ID: `2022.acl-long.324`

DOI: `10.18653/v1/2022.acl-long.324`

Paper page: <https://aclanthology.org/2022.acl-long.324/>

PDF: <https://aclanthology.org/2022.acl-long.324.pdf>

## Note On "Maru 2020"

The requested name was "maru2020", but the benchmark source used in this repository is the 2022 Maru
et al. paper. No distinct Maru 2020 WSD benchmark paper matching the lexEN source artifacts was
identified during this update.
